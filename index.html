OVERVIEW

 

The Computational Perception Lab at Nanjing University develops novel methods for intelligent perception in a wide range of applications, such as biology, astronomy, physics and consumer product, etc. The perception goal ranges from low-level vision information, such as images or videos, to higher-level perception tasks specifically depending on the application. We work with optics, electronics, information theory, biological vision and machine learning, etc. and propose to optimize the computational perception system.
