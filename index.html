[Our Mission]

We are working at the intersection of optics, electronics, signal processing, optimiation and artificial intelligence. We aim at developing the next generation computational imaging technologies, including, Ultra-low Light Imaging, None-Line-of-Sight Imaging, Ultra-fast Imaging and Intelligent Vision System that could facilitate applications over astronomy, biology, security, and consumer product etc.
