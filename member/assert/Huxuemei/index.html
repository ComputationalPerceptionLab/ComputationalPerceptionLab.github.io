About Me

I am now an Associate Researcher in the Computational Perception Lab in Nanjing Univeristy. My research focuses on Computational Imaging, I'm currently focused on Time of Flight imaging and high-speed/ultra-high speed imaging.

I'm currently working with Prof. Tao Yue, and we are leading a group of 8 students working on computational photography and deep learning. For students who are interested and have passion in our research area, welcome to join us!
